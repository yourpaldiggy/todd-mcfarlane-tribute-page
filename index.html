Todd McFarlane

The visionary behind Spawn & Image Comics

Todd McFarlane speaking at the Phoenix Comic Con in Phoenx, Arizona.
Starting in 1988, Todd McFarlane worked on The Amazing Spider-Man at Marvel Comics.

After fantastic covers like Amazing Spider-Man No. 313(which sold for $71,200 in 2010)

Todd started working on his own story line of a new book simply titled Spiderman.

After people complained of his dark writing style, with some stores refusing to even carry the books,

Todd went on to form his own company Image Comics, writing and drawing his own book Spawn
